The DTN Blog

When I started with my research back in 2011, I was quick to get familiar with the Opportunistic Network Environment (ONE) simulator. However, I soon realized the lack of substantial documentation and walthrough. And thus, I began with the DTN blog!

Although I started with Google Sites, later I switched to Blogger. A blog was useful to quickly publish new contents. Moreover, comments from the users also made me feel happy :)

As of now, the DTN blog has, among others, a three-part tutorial on the ONE simulator covering various aspects – the basics, parameterized simulations and confidence intervals. However, more than having a blog, I feel that through this blog I have been able to give back to the research community, which itself had helped me on different queries on a more than occasions.

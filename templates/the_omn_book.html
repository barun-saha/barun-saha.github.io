Opportunistic Mobile Networks: Advances and Applications is our forthcoming book to be published by Springer. This book, #TheOMNBook, if you may say, presents a thoughtful assimilation of the recent advances in OMNs across several dimensions such as, routing, cooperation, heterogeneity, emotions, and more. The book also reflects on different real-life aspects and standards such as, Requests For Comments (RFCs) and patents. A dedicated chapter on network simulation is aimed to help you develop and test protocols with confidence. Additionally, the book also focuses on a few contextual topics in closely related domains, wherever relevant.

The primary objective of #TheOMNBook is to elucidate some of the fundamental challenges in OMNs, and guide you along different paths towards addressing such issues. The chapters have been crafted in a way so that both beginners as well as seasoned researchers would find them useful. These chapters are accompanied with a set of review terms and conceptual as well as programming exercises. Our hope herein is that #TheOMNBook would not only help you gain clear understanding of the fundamental concepts, but also establish you on a firm ground to further extend the contemporary state-of-the-art in OMNs. Opportunistic Mobile Networks: Advances and Applications would be available in print as well as electronic versions in 2016.

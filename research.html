Research Activities
Some of my (current and past) research areas and interests include:
  • Generative AI, Large Language Models, and agentic solutions
  • Intent-based Networks and Systems
  • Delay Tolerant and Opportunistic Networks
  • Mobile Computing and Algorithms

Currently, I primarily work on Generative AI and AI-driven networks and systems. I have worked toward proposing an architecture of Intent-based Networks (IBNs) and explored industrial use cases of IBNs. In this context, I have designed a mechanism for network management and querying using natural language. I have also investigated optimal network flow allocation in the context of industrial/power grid networks.

Previously, I have worked in the domain of Opportunistic Mobile Networks (OMNs), a subclass of Delay Tolerant Networks (DTNs). In my PhD and MS (by Research) theses, I have explored different avenues of content dissemination in OMNs. In addition, I have also worked a bit related to online education technologies.

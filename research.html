My research topic is in the broad area of Delay Tolerant Networks (DTNs). In my MS thesis, I have attempted to explore some previously unaddressed issues in Pocket Switched Networks (PSNs). In particular, I have considered the effects of certain human aspects, innate intelligence and emotions, upon the performance of the network. As such, the otherwise mechanical nodes of a typical network evolve into supernodes, which are a combination of the mobile devices together with their human owner.

I am also interested in other areas of wireless networking, for example, mobile ad hoc networks (MANETs).
